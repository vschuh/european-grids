How to Play

  • Select an empty cell to make a guess for that square.
  • Your chosen player must match the criteria for both the intersecting row and column.
  • You have 9 guesses to fill out the grid. An incorrect guess costs one guess.
  • A player may only be used once on the grid.
  • Good luck!
