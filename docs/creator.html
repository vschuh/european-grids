How to Create Your Grid

  1. Click on any of the six header cells (3 for rows, 3 for columns) to open the category selector.
  2. Choose a category type, then select the specific team, stat, or tournament you want.
  3. You can create your own custom stats by selecting "Create Custom Stat...".
  4. Once all six categories are filled, you can optionally give your grid a name.
  5. Click "Create & Get Share Link" to generate a unique URL for your grid.
